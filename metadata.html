Before uploading photos to the web, ensure you don't give out too much information. Cameras and smartphones store EXIF data, including time, date, camera used, and location. Use this tool to view this data and download an image with all extra information removed.

This process works offline and doesn't upload the photo anywhere.

Browse for your photo, get all information, and click the "Download clean image" link to download the image with all extra data removed.
